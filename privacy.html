Privacy Policy

Keystone Prep High School is committed to protecting the privacy of our students, families, alumni, donors, website visitors, and community members.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information obtained through our website and related communications. By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Student information submitted through admissions inquiries
  • Event registrations
  • Donation information
  • Employment application information
  • Other information you choose to provide through forms or communications

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to provide services, respond to inquiries, process applications, or fulfill requested actions.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of visit
  • Referring website information

This information is used to improve website performance, security, functionality, and user experience.

How We Use Information

Information collected through our website may be used to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Process admissions requests
  • Communicate with prospective and current families
  • Register participants for events
  • Process donations
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Support school operations
  • Comply with legal obligations

We use information only for legitimate educational, operational, and communication purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience and gather website performance information.

These technologies help us understand:

  • How visitors use our website
  • Which pages are most helpful
  • How we can improve navigation and content

Visitors may adjust browser settings to disable cookies, although some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Services

Keystone Prep may utilize trusted third-party service providers to assist with website operations, admissions, fundraising, payment processing, analytics, email communications, and other school functions.

These providers may have access to limited information necessary to perform services on our behalf but are expected to protect that information appropriately.

Examples may include:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Online payment processors
  • Admissions and enrollment platforms
  • Email marketing services

Student Information

Keystone Prep takes the privacy of student information seriously.

Student records and educational information are maintained and protected in accordance with applicable federal and state laws, including those governing educational records and student privacy.

Student information is only shared when required by law, authorized by parents or guardians, or necessary to support educational services and school operations.

Donor Privacy

Keystone Prep respects the privacy of donors and supporters.

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise distribute donor information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Donation information is used solely for processing gifts, maintaining records, providing acknowledgments, and supporting fundraising efforts.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to protect information, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for informational purposes.

Keystone Prep is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of external websites. Visitors should review the privacy policies of those websites before providing personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended primarily for parents, guardians, students, and community members.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through our website without appropriate parental involvement.

If we become aware that information has been submitted inappropriately, we will take reasonable steps to remove it.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Request updates to personal information
  • Opt out of certain communications
  • Request removal from mailing lists
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

Requests may be submitted using the contact information provided below.

Policy Updates

Keystone Prep High School may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements, technology, or school operations.

Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
