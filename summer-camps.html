Archaeology & Art Camp
Featured Camp

Archaeology & Art Camp

June 15–19 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM Middle School Students Space Limited

Students become explorers, artists, and storytellers during this exciting summer experience developed in partnership with USF Tampa. Campers learn excavation techniques, use 3D scanning technology to recreate artifacts, and document discoveries through their own creative comic books.

Led by USF experts, students will:

Explore archaeology and ancient civilizations
Learn excavation techniques
Use 3D scanning technology
Create art projects including weaving and pottery
Develop teamwork and critical thinking skills
Build creativity and confidence

Designed for future archaeologists, artists, and curious thinkers — this camp blends science, technology, history, and creativity into one unforgettable experience.

Mission Control Camp
Featured Camp

Mission Control Camp

July 20–24 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM Space Limited

Get ready for liftoff. Mission Control Camp at Keystone Prep is an exciting hands-on experience for curious minds and future innovators. Campers will build, create, and explore while learning how engineers and scientists think.

Students will:

Build and launch real rockets
Learn drone piloting fundamentals
Design and operate robots
Explore engineering and technology
Develop teamwork and problem-solving skills
Experience hands-on STEM learning

Packed with creativity, discovery, and high-energy activities — Mission Control Camp gives students the opportunity to think like scientists, build like engineers, and create an unforgettable summer experience. Future astronauts and inventors should reserve their spot before launch.
