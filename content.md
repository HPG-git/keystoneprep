# Keystone Prep High School Website Navigation & Conversion Architecture

## Strategy

The website should not follow a traditional school structure focused primarily on information.

Instead, it should guide families through a decision journey:

**Problem → Hope → Proof → Experience → Action**

Most parents arrive asking:

- Will my child fit in?
- Will they receive enough support?
- Can they succeed here?
- How is this different from what we already tried?
- What happens next?

The site architecture should answer those questions naturally while reinforcing:

- Small class sizes (10 average / 16 maximum)
- Individualized support
- Project-based learning
- Real-world preparation
- Student confidence and growth
- Strong parent partnership
