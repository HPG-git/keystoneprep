Our Story

More Than a School

Located in the heart of the Tampa Bay Area, strategically positioned with convenient access from Hillsborough, Pinellas, and Pasco counties, Keystone Prep is dedicated to offering students with learning challenges the highest educational opportunities along with the social and emotional support that will benefit these students far into their futures.

Our Approach

An Individualized Approach to Learning

Due to our small class sizes, ≤16 student per class, our teachers are able to build strong relationships with our students in order to better understand their learning needs and their individual interests.

Beginning in 9th grade students select one of our three magnet tracks, Mission Control, Art Lab, or CSI to focus their studies in broad fields that they are currently interested in. These magnet tracks expose our students to courses aligned with their interests and are tailored to their individual abilities.

Once a semester our students have the opportunity to work on a passion project in our Research class. These projects are chosen by the student and aligned with the student’s chosen magnet track. Our students have the entire semester to work on these projects, which they present to our parent community at our end-of-semester Student Showcase.

Through a careful review of each students Psych-Ed Evaluation, IEP, EP, and/or 504 we pay particular attention to each of our students accommodations to further enhance their educational experience and learning potential.

Our Mission

Why We Exist

Our mission is to offer a project-based magnet program designed for students with learning challenges so that they can flourish academically, emotionally, and socially, empowering them to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

Where We Are Going

Our vision is to set the standard for excellence in educating students with learning challenges, becoming the destination of choice for families and advocates, as well as educators with a passion for serving our student population.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Our Community

Our values shape how we learn, interact, and grow together every day.

Fortitude

We encourage students to face challenges with courage and perseverance, understanding that growth comes from pushing through difficulty rather than avoiding it.

Self-Discipline

We teach students to take responsibility for their actions, manage their impulses, and remain focused on their goals.

Inclusivity

We foster a welcoming environment where every student feels respected, valued, and supported.

Courtesy

We promote kindness, respect, and thoughtful behavior in all interactions.

Integrity

We guide students to be honest, accountable, and committed to doing what is right, even when it is difficult.

Reliability

We encourage students to be dependable, responsible, and trustworthy members of the community.

Curiosity

We inspire students to ask questions, explore new ideas, and develop a lifelong love of learning.

The Keystone Difference

What Makes Keystone Unique

  • Small class sizes averaging approximately 10 students
  • Personalized support and low student-to-counselor ratios
  • Project-based and problem-based learning experiences
  • Three specialized magnet tracks
  • Portfolio assessments and Student Showcase events
  • College, career, and life readiness preparation
  • Strong partnerships with families
  • A supportive community where students are truly known

Helping Students Discover What's Possible

Our goal is not simply to help students graduate.

Our goal is to help them develop confidence, independence, and the skills needed to succeed wherever life takes them next.

Every day, we work to create an environment where students can grow academically, build meaningful relationships, explore their interests, and discover their potential.

That's what makes Keystone Prep more than a school. It's a place where students prepare for life.
