# Visual Art & Design: Odessa — Magnet Program

## Program Overview

**Theme:** Fine Arts  
**Duration:** 4 years (9th–12th grade)  
**Type:** Interest-Focused Magnet Track  
**Capstone Course:** Art Portfolio (Honors)

Visual Art & Design offers students who desire to be lifelong artists the opportunity to focus on a variety of art options based on student interests. The program includes Drawing, Painting, Sculpture, Digital Art & Photography, and the Honors Art Portfolio capstone.
