# CSI: Odessa — Magnet Program

## Program Overview

**Theme:** Crime Scene Investigation  
**Duration:** 4 years (9th–12th grade)  
**Type:** Interest-Focused Magnet Track  
**Capstone Course:** Forensic Science

CSI: Odessa blends the arts and sciences to enable students to develop a deeper understanding of the human condition. The program focuses on evidence collection, archaeological digs, preservation and analysis of artifacts, the in-depth study of human anatomy, critical thinking, and reconstructing events based on evidence and deductive reasoning.
