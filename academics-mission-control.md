# Mission Control: Odessa — Magnet Program

## Program Overview

**Theme:** Space  
**Duration:** 4 years (9th–12th grade)  
**Type:** Interest-Focused Magnet Track

Mission Control: Odessa is a Space-themed magnet program that focuses on advanced physical sciences and mathematics to solve problems of working in Space. Students build and work with robotics, drones, and rockets through hands-on, project-based learning experiences.

Students who complete this program will be eligible to sit for the **FAA licensure test for Unmanned Aircraft Systems** after their 16th birthday.
