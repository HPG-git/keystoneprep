Our Governance

Guiding the Mission of Keystone Prep

As a nonprofit private school, Keystone Prep is governed by a dedicated Board of Trustees who bring diverse expertise and a shared commitment to student success. The Board provides oversight of school operations, financial stewardship, and long-range strategic planning.

Trustees serve as advocates for the Keystone Prep community — ensuring the school remains focused on its core mission of helping every student succeed through personalized academics and a supportive learning environment.
