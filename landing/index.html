A Different Approach

When Traditional Schools Aren't Working

Many of our students are bright, capable, and ready to do their best work — they just learn differently. Keystone is a nonprofit private school built specifically for students managing ADHD, anxiety, depression, dyslexia, dysgraphia, executive functioning challenges, and similar learning differences.

We provide the structure, support, and relationships students need to succeed — not a one-size-fits-all classroom, but an environment shaped around how each student actually learns.
